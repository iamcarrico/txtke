Donate to TKE

Help support the brotherhood

In order to operate, a Fraternity needs support from its members and alumni. Even though membership dues cover most of our costs, it does not necessarily cover all necessities. To remedy the problem, we ask for support through donations.

We accept monetary or physical donations. To donate, either mail or visit us at the chapter house located at 2525 Nueces Street in Austin, Tx 78705. Please make checks payable to TKE - Gamma Upsilon. We also accept credit card payment through PayPal by clicking the link below. Thank you.
